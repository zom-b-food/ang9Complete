Welcome to the Customers Ang9 App

This is the Home Component which is actually quite simple. Clicking the "Customers" link will take you to the customers section which not so simple as it's over-engineered in the typical Angular way. :)

Don't get me wrong, however. It's not nearly as over-engineered as, say, React! Now that's a real headache!

I could have done this entire app in java/jsp in half the time and half the code. But, whatever, right? This is what everyone wants nowadays, so we all just need to quit fussing about it and get on board.

Until next month when something else is the best way ever to build a webapp.

Anyway, I hope you like it. And feel free to download it on github.
